Interactive step-by-step simulator of Go GMP scheduler (local runqueue, global runqueue, netpoller, work stealing, sysmon preemption) and Go concurrent tricolor marking with hybrid write barrier (Dijkstra + Yuasa).

GMP Scheduler Control Scheduler

Scheduling Rules:
- M0 and M1 threads execute Gs by binding to P0 and P1. P0 and P1 have local queues of capacity 256.
- If P0's queue runs out, clicking 'Work Stealing' steals Gs from P1.
- Syscalls block the thread, causing P to detach from M. Sysmon preempts Gs running >10ms (represented by G running status).
